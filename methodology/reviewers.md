# Reviewer Qualifications

Applies to all human review tasks in this analysis unless a specific review task notes otherwise.

## Criteria

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Number of reviewers | 2 (paper authors; see individual review files) |
| Language | Fluent English |
| Education | Minimum Master's degree |
| Domain experience | 10-15 years of experience in international development or related field, with focus on innovation a plus |
| Institutional familiarity | Familiarity with WBG operations and IEG evaluations preferred |
| LLM familiarity | Limited to base familiarity (reviewers were not expected to have technical AI expertise) |

## Notes

- Tasks involving editorial judgment (Steps 9, 10, 16) were carried out by the authors and are not formalized as coded review tasks.
